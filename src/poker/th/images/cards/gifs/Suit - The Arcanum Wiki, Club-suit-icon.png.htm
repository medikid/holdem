        There are four suits appearing in a tarot deck: Swords (also called Blades or Athames), Wands (also called Batons, Rods, Staffs, or Clubs), Cups (also called Chalices or Goblets), and Pentacles (also called Discs). Each of the suits actually corresponds with a suit in the standard playing card. Each suit consists of fourteen cards, and all four suits combined make up the Minor Arcana (all the cards minus the 22 Major Arcana). The cards are numbered 1 (Ace) through 10, followed by the four Court Cards.
